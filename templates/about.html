About PDF Splitter

Simple, Secure, and Free

Our Mission

We built PDF Splitter with one goal in mind: to provide a free, fast, and secure way to split PDF documents without compromising your privacy.

How It Works

Our tool runs on a secure environment where your files are processed in real-time. Whether you need to extract specific pages, split into equal chunks, or divide by file size, our powerful processing engine handles it all.

Why Choose Us?

  • Split by Size: Provide a target file size (e.g., 10MB) and we'll split your PDF perfectly, without reducing image quality (unlike "compression" tools).
  • No 50MB Limits: While others cap you at 50MB, we support files up to 200MB for free.
  • Privacy First: We operate a strict Zero Retention Policy.
  • Fast: Optimized for speed with minimal wait times.
  • Free: No hidden costs or subscriptions.
