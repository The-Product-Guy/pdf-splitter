Privacy Policy

Your Data stays Yours

Zero Retention Policy

We take your privacy seriously. We do not store your files.

When you upload a PDF to be split:

  • The file is processed in temporary memory.
  • Once the split is complete and you download the result, the files are permanently deleted from our servers immediately.
  • We do not keep backups or logs of your file contents.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. We may collect anonymous usage statistics (like number of pages processed) to improve our service, but this is never linked to your identity or file content.

Security

All transfers are encrypted using SSL/TLS. Our infrastructure is designed to be ephemeral, ensuring no data persists beyond the lifespan of your request.
