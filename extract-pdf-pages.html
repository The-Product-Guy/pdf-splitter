Extract PDF Pages and Page Ranges

Pull out the exact sections you need from a PDF, such as pages 1-5, 8-10, or everything from page 15 onward.

Extract PDF Pages Choose "Split by ranges" in the tool.

When to extract PDF page ranges

Range extraction is useful when a large PDF contains several documents in one file, when you only need a chapter from a study packet, or when an application asks for one section of a bigger document bundle.

Range examples

  • 1-5 extracts pages 1 through 5.
  • 8-10 extracts pages 8 through 10.
  • 15- extracts page 15 through the end of the PDF.
  • 1-5, 8-10 creates separate files for both ranges.

How to extract pages privately

  1. Open PDF Splitter.
  2. Select your PDF.
  3. Choose Split by ranges.
  4. Enter the ranges you want.
  5. Download the extracted files.
Privacy note: The selected PDF is processed locally in your browser. The page ranges you enter are not sent to our server for the main splitting workflow.

FAQ

Can I extract a single page?

Yes. Enter a single page number, such as 7, in the range field.

Can I extract multiple separate ranges?

Yes. Use commas, for example 1-3, 10-12, 20.

Does extracting pages upload the PDF?

No. The main extraction workflow runs in your browser.
