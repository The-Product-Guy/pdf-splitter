About PDF Splitter

Simple, Secure, and Free

Our Mission

We built PDF Splitter with one goal in mind: to provide a free, fast, and secure way to split PDF documents without compromising your privacy.

How It Works

Everything happens right in your browser. When you select a PDF, it is processed locally on your device using JavaScript — your files are never uploaded to any server. This means there are no file size limits and your documents remain completely private.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Browser-Based: Your files never leave your device. No uploads, no servers, no risk.
  • Split by Size: Provide a target file size (e.g., 10MB) and we'll split your PDF perfectly, without reducing image quality.
  • No File Size Limits: Since processing happens locally, there are no upload restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Zero data transmission. Your files stay on your device.
  • Fast: No upload/download wait times. Processing starts instantly.
  • Free: No hidden costs or subscriptions.
