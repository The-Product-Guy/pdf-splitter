Privacy Policy

Your Files Never Leave Your Device

100% Browser-Based Processing

We take your privacy seriously. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

When you use PDF Splitter:

  • All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript.
  • Your PDF files never leave your device — there is no upload step.
  • No file data is transmitted over the network.
  • Once you close or refresh the page, all processed data is cleared from browser memory.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or file content. We may use anonymous analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand usage patterns, but this is never linked to your identity or file content.

Security

Since your files never leave your device, there is no risk of interception during transfer. The website itself is served over HTTPS to ensure the integrity of the application code.
